About the author

Liam Carter has always believed in the magic of stories. Growing up in a quiet town nestled between rolling hills and endless forests, he spent his childhood lost in the pages of books, dreaming of distant worlds, unsolved mysteries, and unforgettable characters. Writing started as a personal escape—a secret world of scribbled notebooks and late-night musings—but soon, it became his greatest passion. After earning a degree in Literature and Creative Writing, Liam embarked on a journey to bring his stories to life. His debut novel, Whispers of the Forgotten, was a runaway success, captivating readers with its blend of mystery, adventure, and deeply human emotions. Since then, he has continued to craft tales that challenge, inspire, and transport his audience into realms unknown. When he’s not writing, Liam enjoys exploring new cities, collecting vintage typewriters, and sipping coffee at hidden bookstores. He believes that every story—like every person—has something extraordinary to offer, and he’s on a mission to share as many as he can with the world.
