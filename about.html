About Us

What we do

Around The Atlas is your compass to the world's most captivating journeys, charting paths beyond the well-trodden since its inception in 2012. Born from the shared passion of Liam Carter and Kai Strom, two individuals driven by the power of stories and the allure of exploration. Liam, a seasoned author and storyteller, whose debut novel, "Whispers of the Forgotten," captivated readers with its blend of adventure and deeply human emotion, brings his unique narrative perspective to the world of travel. Kai, a documentarian, brings the visual experience. Together they aimed to create a publication that celebrated in-depth cultural exploration and the magic found in every journey.
Under a revitalized vision since 2021, Around The Atlas has broadened its horizons, delving into the heart of local communities, uncovering hidden historical gems, and embracing the vibrant pulse of contemporary urban life. We remain steadfast in our commitment to promoting ethical, eco-conscious, and enriching travel experiences, fostering a deeper connection between travelers and the destinations they explore. Now reaching readers across North America, Europe, and Australia, Around The Atlas inspires a global community of mindful explorers.
Our dynamic digital platform, aroundtheatlas.com, serves as a gateway to a wealth of travel inspiration, featuring in-depth articles, practical guides, and stunning visuals, attracting a thriving online audience of over 5.5 million visitors annually, alongside a dedicated social media community of 250,000+ followers. The 'Atlas Collective' provides a space for like-minded travelers to share stories, exchange insights, and forge lasting connections.
Around The Atlas is celebrated for its compelling storytelling, insightful reviews, and actionable travel advice, earning accolades from leading industry organizations like the "Global Travel Journalism Awards" in 2023 for best cultural reporting. Around The Atlas stands as a leading voice in travel journalism, offering a unique blend of authentic narratives and responsible travel advocacy, empowering readers to explore the world with curiosity and respect

Our Story

Welcome to Around The Atlas, your ultimate travel magazine dedicated to inspiring wanderlust and uncovering the beauty of the world. Our journey began with a simple passion for exploration and storytelling. From breathtaking landscapes to hidden gems, we bring you captivating travel experiences, cultural insights, and expert tips to help you embark on unforgettable adventures. Join us as we navigate the globe, one story at a time.

Our Mission

At Around The Atlas, our mission is to ignite a passion for travel by bringing the world closer to our readers. We strive to uncover hidden gems, share cultural insights, and provide expert travel tips that inspire unforgettable journeys. Through captivating storytelling and breathtaking visuals, we aim to transform every reader into an explorer, fostering a deeper appreciation for the beauty, diversity, and adventure that our planet has to offer.

Future Goals

Expand Global Reach

Establish dedicated editorial teams in key regions like South America and Africa. Translate content into multiple languages to cater to a wider international audience. Increase print and digital distribution to emerging travel markets.

Enhance Digital Engagement

Develop interactive travel planning tools and virtual reality experiences on the website. Launch a podcast series featuring interviews with travelers, experts, and locals. Create a mobile app for on-the-go access to articles, maps, and community forums. Increase social media engagement, and create more video content.

Strengthen Community Focus

Organize exclusive travel experiences and workshops for 'Atlas Collective' members. Facilitate online and offline meetups for travelers to connect and share experiences. Establish a grant program to support local tourism initiatives in developing countries.

Champion Sustainable Travel

Develop a comprehensive carbon offset program for travelers and the magazine's operations. Partner with organizations promoting responsible tourism practices. Increase coverage of eco-friendly accommodations and transportation options. Create a system of scoring travel destinations, based on their sustainability.
